ABC Technologies Employee Policy Handbook

--------------------------------------------------
1. COMPANY OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------

ABC Technologies is a software consulting and digital transformation company.
The company provides cloud, AI, data engineering, and application development services to clients worldwide.

--------------------------------------------------
2. WORKING HOURS
--------------------------------------------------

Employees are expected to work 8 hours per day.

Official working hours are:
9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (Monday to Friday)

Employees are allowed flexible login between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM provided they complete their daily work hours.

--------------------------------------------------
3. WORK FROM HOME POLICY
--------------------------------------------------

Employees may work from home up to 3 days per week with manager approval.

Remote employees must:

- Attend all scheduled meetings
- Be available during core hours (10:00 AM to 4:00 PM)
- Maintain a stable internet connection
- Follow all information security guidelines

--------------------------------------------------
4. LEAVE POLICY
--------------------------------------------------

Employees receive the following leave benefits each year:

- 20 Paid Leave (PL)
- 10 Sick Leave (SL)
- 10 Casual Leave (CL)

Unused Paid Leave can be carried forward up to 10 days into the next year.

Sick Leave cannot be carried forward.

Leave requests should be submitted at least 2 days in advance whenever possible.

--------------------------------------------------
5. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
--------------------------------------------------

Employees can claim reimbursement for:

- Business travel
- Client meetings
- Training programs approved by management
- Internet expenses up to ₹1,500 per month

Expense claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date.

Receipts are mandatory for all claims above ₹500.

--------------------------------------------------
6. INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY
--------------------------------------------------

Employees must:

- Use company-approved devices
- Enable multi-factor authentication
- Never share passwords
- Lock systems when away from desks
- Report security incidents immediately

Sharing confidential customer information with unauthorized persons is strictly prohibited.

Violation of security policies may lead to disciplinary action.

--------------------------------------------------
7. DRESS CODE POLICY
--------------------------------------------------

Business casual attire is required when visiting client locations.

Employees may wear smart casuals while working from company offices.

Clothing that contains offensive language or images is not permitted.

--------------------------------------------------
8. TRAINING AND LEARNING
--------------------------------------------------

All employees are encouraged to complete at least 20 hours of training annually.

ABC Technologies provides access to:

- AI and Machine Learning courses
- Cloud certifications
- Technical workshops
- Leadership development programs

The company reimburses certification costs for approved certifications upon successful completion.

--------------------------------------------------
9. PERFORMANCE REVIEW
--------------------------------------------------

Performance reviews are conducted twice a year.

Employees are evaluated on:

- Technical skills
- Quality of work
- Teamwork
- Customer satisfaction
- Innovation

Outstanding performers may be eligible for bonuses and promotions.

--------------------------------------------------
10. HEALTH INSURANCE
--------------------------------------------------

Full-time employees are eligible for company-sponsored health insurance.

Coverage includes:

- Employee
- Spouse
- Up to two dependent children

The annual insurance coverage amount is ₹5,00,000.

--------------------------------------------------
11. CODE OF CONDUCT
--------------------------------------------------

Employees must maintain professional behavior at all times.

Harassment, discrimination, and workplace violence are strictly prohibited.

Employees should treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect.

Any violation of the code of conduct may result in disciplinary action.
